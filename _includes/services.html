Features

Dashboard

A Dashboard that displays the electrical parameters of the coil. The dashboard is accessed via the internet and data is securely stored in the "cloud".

Coil Monitoring

The coils are monitored remotely when not in use once they are placed on GaDova's proprietary platform.

Failure Prediction

The ability to predict soft and hard failures including image quality deterioration and cable and connector failures.

Communication

End users (clinical and engineering) are informed of a failure of an MRI coil via text and email before the coil is placed inside the bore of the magnet.
